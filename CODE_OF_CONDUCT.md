# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project expects respectful, constructive collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Give technical feedback with concrete evidence.
- Assume good intent, question ideas, not people.
- Help keep discussions focused and actionable.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Repeated bad-faith argumentation or disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants for violations.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and related project spaces.
